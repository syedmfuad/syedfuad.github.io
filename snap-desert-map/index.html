Definition

A SNAP desert is a census tract where ≥15% of households receive SNAP benefits AND the nearest full-service grocery store is >0.5 mi away (urban) or >10 mi away (rural).

Full-service stores: Supermarkets, Large Grocery Stores, and Super Stores only. Convenience stores and specialty retailers are excluded from the access calculation.
